How to Make Vietnamese Bánh Mì

  • Step 1. Buy a French baguette.
  • Step 2. Spread Vietnamese mayo on the bottom of the bread.
  • Step 3. Add in choice of meat or tofu.
  • Step 4. Top it with Vietnamese pickled carrots and daikon. Add in cilantro, cucumber, and some pieces of jalapeno.
  • Step 5. Slice sandwich into three.
